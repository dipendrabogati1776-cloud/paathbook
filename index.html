About PaathBook

PaathBook is an educational learning app designed for school students and self-learners who prefer studying in Nepali and English.

The app focuses on simplicity, readability, and a distraction-free learning experience.

Key Features

  • Educational content in Nepali and English
  • PDF notes and study materials
  • General Knowledge (GK) lessons and quizzes
  • GK multiple-choice questions (MCQs) for practice
  • Simple and student-friendly dictionary
  • No login or signup required
  • Student-friendly and easy-to-use interface
  • Privacy-focused design

Privacy First

PaathBook respects user privacy and does not directly collect or store personal information.

The app is designed to be safe for students and follows Google Play and AdMob policies.
