About

A reading-first language learning application.

Paathbook Bhasa helps learners improve naturally through reading, listening and interactive practice instead of memorizing random words.

Stories, dialogs and vocabulary are connected to an instant dictionary, pronunciation support and revision games, so every reading session becomes language practice.

FAQ

Questions learners usually ask first.

What languages are supported?

Paathbook Bhasa supports English, Nepali, Hindi, Japanese and Korean.

Does the app work offline?

The app is designed with an offline-first architecture so learning content can remain available where supported.

Can I learn multiple languages?

Yes. You can choose a known language and a learning language, then switch learning paths as needed.

Is pronunciation available?

Yes. Paathbook Bhasa includes pronunciation support for vocabulary and learning content.

How does Vocabulary Adventure work?

Users unlock themed levels, learn vocabulary, and complete practice games to reinforce what they studied.
