PAATHBOOK BHASA Privacy Policy

PAATHBOOK BHASA respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when using our application.

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PAATHBOOK BHASA.

By using the application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

At this time, PAATHBOOK BHASA collects very little personal information.

The app may store the following locally on your device:

  • Learning progress
  • Saved vocabulary
  • Game history
  • User language preferences
  • Offline learning data
This information remains on your device unless future cloud synchronization is enabled.

3. Personal Information

Currently, PAATHBOOK BHASA does not require users to create an account.

We do not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Government identification

4. Permissions

PAATHBOOK BHASA currently does not request sensitive device permissions such as:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Location
  • SMS
  • Call logs
  • Calendar
If future versions require additional permissions, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are introduced.

5. Internet Usage

The application may access the internet to:

  • Retrieve dictionary information when a word is unavailable locally.
  • Improve language learning features.
  • Download future content updates.

Internet access is not used to collect unnecessary personal information.

6. AI Services

Some language explanations or dictionary entries may be generated using third-party AI services when they are not available locally.

Only the text necessary to generate the requested explanation or dictionary entry is sent.

We do not intentionally send personally identifiable information to AI providers.

7. Local Storage

The app stores learning information locally, including:

  • Saved words
  • Reading progress
  • Adventure progress
  • Game statistics
  • User settings

This information is stored only to improve your learning experience.

8. Third-Party Services

The application may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Firebase (future authentication, cloud sync)
  • Google Gemini API (dictionary fallback)
  • Google Play Services (application distribution)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy.

9. Children's Privacy

PAATHBOOK BHASA is designed for learners of all ages.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe personal information has been provided by a child, please contact us so we can address the issue.

10. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data stored within the application.

However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure.

11. Future Features

Future versions of PAATHBOOK BHASA may introduce features such as:

  • User accounts
  • Cloud synchronization
  • Premium subscriptions
  • Website login
  • Progress synchronization across devices

If these features change how information is collected or processed, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

The latest version will always be available at:

https://paathbook.com/bhasa/privacy-policy
