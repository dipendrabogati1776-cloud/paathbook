Privacy Policy

PaathBook respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the PaathBook mobile application.

PaathBook is designed for school students and focuses on providing a safe, simple, and privacy-friendly learning experience.

Last updated: Jan 3, 2026

Information We Collect

PaathBook does not collect any personal information from users.

  • No name, email, or phone number
  • No location data
  • No login or signup required

Internet Usage

PaathBook requires an internet connection to:

  • Load educational content
  • Display advertisements
  • Fetch learning materials such as PDF notes

No personal data is transmitted or stored during this process.

Advertisements

PaathBook displays ads through third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob).

These services may use anonymous, non-personal information to display ads in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Permissions

PaathBook does not request any sensitive device permissions.

PDF files viewed in the app may be temporarily downloaded and stored securely within the app’s private storage for performance purposes. These files are not accessible outside the app, and no storage permission is required.

Children’s Privacy

PaathBook is intended for school students.

  • No personal data is knowingly collected from children
  • No accounts or social features are included
  • Parents can safely allow children to use the app

Third-Party Services

PaathBook may use third-party services for advertisements and content delivery. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
